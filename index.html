About me.

Kai received his BEng and MEng degrees from Hangzhou Dianzi University, Hangzhou, China, in 2007 and 2020, respectively. Since 2019, Kai worked at the Emerging Devices Technologies research group of the University of Birmingham as a visiting research student. Currently, Kai is working as a research assistant at Prof Xin Yao's Group of the Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech). And, Kai is a PhD student of the University of Technology Sydney with research interests including microwave, millimeter-wave and terahertz circuits and antennas. Kai is a student member of the Chinese Institute of Electronics, and a reviewer of the IEEE ACCESS.

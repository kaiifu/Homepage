ABOUT ME

I was born in Jinzhong, Shanxi, China and received BEng and MEng degrees from Hangzhou Dianzi University, Hangzhou, China, in 2007 and 2020, respectively.

During my master‘s study, I was supervised by Professor Wen-Sheng Zhao with research interests focused on signal integrity of 3D ICs, design and optimization of TSV based interconnects and electronics design automation (EDA) using neural-networks.

Since 2019, I was a visiting research student of the University of Birmingham. During this period, I am working at the Emerging Devices Technologies research group with an interest focused on synthesis and design of RF acoustic wave filters.

Since 2020, I was a visiting research student and also a research assistant of the Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech). During this period, I am working at the Prof Xin YAO's group with an interest focused on Machine Learning Assisted Evolutionary Algorithm for Antenna Design Optimization.

Currently, I am a PhD student working at the State Key Laboratory of Terahertz and Millimeter Waves (City University of Hong Kong) with research interests including AI inspired Antenna, Metasurface and Metamaterial Design.

I am a student member of IEEE and the Chinese Institute of Electronics, and a reviewer of the IEEE ACCESS.
